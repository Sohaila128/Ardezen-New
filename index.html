ARDEZEN Washing Tips
We recommend using ARDEZEN PPF CARE SET, available for online purchase at ARDEZEN WEBSITE (www.ardezen.net). Make sure the vehicle is cool to the touch & avoid washing in direct sun. Use dedicated wash mitt, 2 bucket system with filters & a couple of microfiber towels for the panels protected by ARDEZEN PPF COLLECTION. For best results, wash & dry ONE section at a time. Always wash the vehicle from TOP to BOTTOM. Always rinse the wash mitt frequently to release debris, replace the water in the bucket frequently.

ARDEZEN Washing Instructions
Add 3 cupful (45mk / 1.5oz) of ARDEZEN NOURISHING SHAMPOO in the bucket. Fill half of the bucket (11 Liter / 2.5gal) of warm water with short stream to activate the suds. Rinse ONE section of the vehicle thoroughly with water. Gently glide ARDEZEN WASH MITT with no added pressure on the panel. Rinse the section thoroughly, DONâ€™T allow the ARDEZEN NOURISHING SHMAPOO to dry. Use the SHEETING METHOD to dry most of the water followed by 2 dedicated ARDEZEN DRYING MF TOWELS. Repeat the above steps for the next section.
